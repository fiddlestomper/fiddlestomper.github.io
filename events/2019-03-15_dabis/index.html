Concert @ Dabis Kaffeestube, Göttingen

About the event

  • What? Concert
  • When? 2019-03-15
  • Where? Dabis Kaffeestube, Göttingen
  • How much? Free, donation appreciated

On the Friday, March 15th, I’ll have the great pleasure to play in Dabis Kaffeestube, Rote Straße 39, in Göttingen.

The concert is at 18:00. The entrance is free and a hat will be passed around at the end of the concert.

Hope to see you there!
