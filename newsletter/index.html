Subscribe to Fiddlestomer's Newsletter

If you want to stay on top of any news, videos, photos, or events, you can subscribe to my newsletter. You'll then be the first to know about upcoming events and workshops, and you'll be receiving a curated collection of my videos, photos, random thoughts, and every now and then recommendations on music I like and enjoy.

If you hate spam, don't worry. I do too. There will be a limited amount of emails. Unless there are events coming up, you'll probably receive less than one email each month. And if you want to unsubscribe, you can find a link to do that in each of the emails I send you.

The emails are sent out via MailChimp, and by subscribing you'll add your email to a mailing list to which only I have access. If you want to be removed from this list, just unsubscribe, or contact me and I'll remove you.
